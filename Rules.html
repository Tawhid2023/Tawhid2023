Rules for blood donors :

Bangladeshi citizens will register as blood donors with correct information. Must add a active mobile number.
After donating all the information and successfully completing the registration, you will be added to the database as a blood donor. Information about blood donation must be updated in your profile.

Blood recipients will find you if they needed blood. Your mobile number, name, location that given by you will be published to them. They will contact you on that number. If you have an opportunity to donate blood, give blood to the blood recipient.

Know before you go to donate blood


Donate blood to ensure your safety. In case of any problems , the Bangladesh Blood Bank Authority will not be held responsible in any way.

01. Drink two to three glasses more water than usual. Avoid soft drinks or juices instead of water just before blood donation.
02. Knowing the location of the patient, go to the hospital to donate blood.
03. Do not go to donate blood anywhere except hospital, clinic. Don't go to his house even if blood recipient told you , not in any alley.
04. Look at the patient's report, the doctor's requisition letter.
05. Take a look at the patient.
06. Donate blood after cross-mixing.
07. Be sure to use the new needle.
07. Inform the authorities if in doubt about the expertise of the experts.
09. Let them know that you are donating blood voluntarily so that third parties do not get a chance to commit corruption.
10. If you feel sick for any reason, refrain from giving blood like that day.
11. Eat nutritious food before donating blood, but do not eat anything oily. Eat foods rich in iron such as watermelon, spinach or other vegetables rich in iron. Eat foods rich in vitamin C such as juice, amalki or other vitamin C rich fruits.
13. Sleep well the night before the day you donate blood.

Blood donation is a great and selfish thing. So don't give in to emotions, be aware of yourself and give blood to others.

Blood Applicant / Patient Actions:


The blood donor is voluntarily coming forward at your peril. So it is very important to treat him with safety and gratitude. Things you must try to obey. The blood donor is voluntarily coming forward at your peril. So it is very important to treat him with safety and gratitude. Things you must try to obey.

01. The blood donor is coming to help you voluntarily in your need, so you will bear the cost of his travel.
02. Arrange for his rest after blood donation.
03. Arrange nutritious food and fruits as much as possible. Because it is important for the recovery of the blood donor's body.
04. Have a good relationship with him after he recovers. Remember he is your friend in danger. His blood is flowing in your body.
05. Try to be by his side in danger.
